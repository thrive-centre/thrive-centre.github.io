# Biography
I work at the intersection of medicine, research, and technology, focusing on how data‑driven approaches can enhance clinical decision‑making and improve patient outcomes. My background in emergency medicine and medical education has given me a deep understanding of complex, high‑pressure clinical environments and how smarter systems can support both clinicians and patients.
I am particularly interested in the practical application of AI and digital innovation in healthcare, with an emphasis on solutions that make care safer, faster, and more accessible. I value interdisciplinary teamwork, clear communication, and building meaningful connections across clinical and academic settings.
