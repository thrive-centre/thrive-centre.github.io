## Project Overview

We are building an AI model that automatically traces the left-ventricular (LV) endocardial border in apical four-chamber (A4CH) echocardiographic images.

The contour follows a consistent anatomical path: from one basal point, down through the apex, and back to the opposite basal point.

This project aims to produce clear, reliable LV borders that support clinical measurements and improve the consistency of echocardiographic analysis.
